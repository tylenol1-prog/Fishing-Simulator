# Fishing-Simulator
a super fun fishing sim!



Features:

🎣 Cast mechanic: Hold SPACE to build power, then click to cast at different angles
🐟 Fish AI: Multiple fish species (Bass, Trout, Catfish, Goldfish) swimming around with realistic behavior
🎯 Hook system: Wait for fish to bite, then press SPACE to set the hook and reel them in
📊 Scoring system: Different fish are worth different points
🎨 Simple graphics: Clean 2D visuals with water and sky
How to play:

Hold SPACE to charge your cast power
Move the mouse to aim
Release SPACE to cast
Wait for fish to bite (bobber will dip)
Press SPACE when a fish bites to reel it in
Press R to reset the game
